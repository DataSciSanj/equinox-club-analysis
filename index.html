Equinox Club Analysis Report

Insights from Demographic and Income Data

Introduction

This report presents an analysis of the Equinox Club data, focusing on demographic and income variables. The goal is to uncover insights that can help inform business strategy and decision-making.

Data Overview

The dataset used in this analysis was compiled from various sources, including Equinox club location data and U.S. Census data on median age and income distributions. The data has been cleaned and imputed to ensure completeness and accuracy for analysis.

Methodology

The analysis employs exploratory data analysis (EDA), including histograms and bar charts, to understand the distribution of key variables. A K-Means clustering algorithm is then applied to segment the data into distinct groups based on demographic and income variables.

Findings & Insights

Based on the cluster analysis, six distinct groups were identified. These clusters reveal patterns in demographic and income characteristics, which can be leveraged for targeted marketing and strategic planning.

Conclusion

The analysis highlights key demographic and income trends that are relevant to the business. Future work could involve refining the clusters further or exploring additional variables that may impact club membership and profitability.
