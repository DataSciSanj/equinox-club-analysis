Equinox Club Analysis: Demographic and Financial Insights

Welcome to the Equinox Club Analysis project, where we explore the distribution and characteristics of Equinox clubs across various locations, analyzing demographic and financial data to uncover patterns and insights.

Data Overview

Datasets Used

This analysis uses data scraped from the Equinox website, combined with demographic data sourced from the U.S. Census and ACS.

Data Cleaning & Preprocessing

The data was cleaned to remove duplicates, handle missing values, and standardize formats. Outliers in income data were treated to prevent skewing the analysis.

Visualizations

Clustering Analysis

Approach

K-Means clustering was applied to segment the clubs based on demographic and income variables. The optimal number of clusters was determined using the Elbow Method.

Cluster Descriptions

We identified multiple customer segments, each with distinct demographic characteristics.

Key Findings & Insights

Income Distribution Analysis

The analysis revealed that clubs are primarily located in high-income areas, suggesting a target demographic of affluent individuals. Additionally, clusters showed differences in median age and income, indicating diverse market segments.

Conclusion

This project provided valuable insights into the demographic and financial characteristics of Equinox club locations, offering strategic recommendations to better align with target markets.
